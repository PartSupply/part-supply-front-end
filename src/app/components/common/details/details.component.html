Shops and other professionals need part(s) based on brand, cost, part condition, warranty and delivery time.
Sellers have the part(s) and ability to fullfill the order(s).
partsupply.us helps supply the demand by reducing time spent searching the web
and talking on the phone - so you can keep business flowing.

Sellers

CREATE A SELLER ACCOUNT -
Within 24 hours, your Seller account will be completed and you will receive an email notification to begin selling.

After you have received your Welcome notice, you may LOGIN using the email and the password you have selected for the portal.

VIEW BUYER REQUEST FOR PARTS WITHIN YOUR SET RADIUS:

- Each offer you make shall include: Part(s) as described AND includes the fees for local delivery or shipment.

WATCH FOR THUMBS UP OR THUMBS DOWN TO DETERMINE IF YOUR OFFER IS THE BEST OFFER IN CURRENT STANDING OR IF ANOTHER SELLER HAS MADE A BETTER OFFER. WHEN THE BUYER SELECTS AN OFFER, ONLY THE SELLER THEY HAVE ACCEPTED RECEIVES "OFFER ACCEPTED".

WHEN YOU RECEIVE "OFFER ACCEPTED" FROM A BUYER, prepare the order and delivery / shipment immediately.

PAYMENT IS PROCESSED UPON DELIVERY, AS IN DONG BUSINESS AS USUAL.

Buyers

CREATE A BUYER ACCOUNT -
Within 24 hours, your Buyer account will be completed and you will receive an email notification, so you can begin requesting parts.

After you have received your Welcome notice, you may LOGIN using the email and the password you have selected for the portal.

POST A REQUEST FOR PARTS:

- The price(s) posted from Sellers include the fees for local delivery or shipment to your address.

YOU MAY RECEIVE OFFERS FROM A SELLER OR MULTIPLE SELLERS. WHEN YOU SELECT AN OFFER, THE SELLER WILL RECEIVE "OFFER ACCEPTED" AND PROCESS YOUR ORDER. YOUR PART IS ON ITS WAY TO YOU. partsupply.us DOES NOT PROCESS YOUR PAYMENT. ALL PAYMENTS ARE HANDLED DIRECTLY BY THE SELLER.
